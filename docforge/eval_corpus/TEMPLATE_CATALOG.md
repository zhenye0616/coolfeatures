# DocForge Evaluation Corpus: Document Template Catalog

> **Purpose**: Catalog of 85 real-world document templates for evaluating DocForge's template extraction pipeline.
> Each entry includes variable fields, complexity rating, and sources for obtaining `.docx` examples.

---

## 1. Legal (12 templates)

### 1.1 Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — Unilateral
- **Variable fields**: Disclosing party name/address, receiving party name/address, effective date, definition of confidential information, term/duration, governing state/jurisdiction, signature blocks
- **Complexity**: Simple (8-12 fields)
- **Sources**: [eForms](https://eforms.com/nda/), [Vertex42](https://www.vertex42.com/WordTemplates/non-disclosure-agreement.html), [LegalTemplates.net](https://legaltemplates.net/form/non-disclosure-agreement/)

### 1.2 Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — Mutual
- **Variable fields**: Party A name/address, Party B name/address, effective date, mutual confidential information definitions, exclusions, term, remedies, governing law, signatures
- **Complexity**: Medium (12-18 fields)
- **Sources**: [eForms](https://eforms.com/nda/), [Vertex42](https://www.vertex42.com/WordTemplates/non-disclosure-agreement.html), [PandaDoc](https://www.pandadoc.com/nda-template/)

### 1.3 Independent Contractor Agreement
